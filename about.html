About Us

Naloxone Advocates Plymouth CIC is a peer‑led community organisation dedicated to reducing drug‑related deaths in Plymouth and Devon. We deliver naloxone training, harm reduction support, and work directly with local communities.

  • Peer‑led, lived‑experience approach
  • Free community training
  • Stigma‑free support
  • Committed to saving lives through harm reduction
