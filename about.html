About Us

Naloxone Advocates Plymouth CIC is a community‑led organisation focused on reducing drug‑related deaths through naloxone training, harm reduction and peer support.

  • Peer‑led, lived‑experience approach
  • Stigma‑free training and support
  • Accessible sessions for individuals, groups and organisations
