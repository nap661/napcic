How to Get Naloxone

  1. Attend one of our free naloxone training sessions.
  2. Learn to recognise an overdose and administer naloxone safely.
  3. Receive your free naloxone kit at the end of training.

Always call 999 in an emergency. Naloxone is a temporary measure—professional help is essential.
